# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## General Questions

### 1. What is MyChild Helpline?

MyChild Helpline is a mobile application that provides users with access to support services, educational resources, articles, workshops, and counseling-related activities designed to promote child welfare and well-being.

### 2. Who can use MyChild Helpline?

The application is intended for children, parents, guardians, caregivers, and other individuals seeking information, support services, and educational resources.

### 3. Is an internet connection required to use the application?

Some features may be available offline; however, an active internet connection is required to access online services, articles, workshops, and updated content.

## Account and Access

### 4. I cannot log in to my account. What should I do?

Verify that your login credentials are correct. If the issue persists, use the password recovery feature or contact the appropriate support channel.

### 5. How can I update my account information?

Navigate to your profile settings and update the necessary information. Ensure that changes are saved before exiting the page.

### 6. I forgot my password. What should I do?

Select the **Forgot Password** option on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

## Blog and Online Services

### 7. Why are articles not loading in the Blog section?

Ensure that your device is connected to the internet and refresh the page. If the issue continues, restart the application and try again.

### 8. Why can't I access MyChild Online Services?

Verify your internet connection and ensure that the application is updated to the latest version.

### 9. Are new articles and workshops added regularly?

Yes. New articles, workshops, and other resources may be added periodically to provide users with updated information and support services.

## Application Issues

### 10. The application is running slowly. What should I do?

Close unused applications, verify your internet connection, and restart the application.

### 11. The application is not responding. What should I do?

Close the application completely and reopen it. If the issue persists, restart your device and try again.

### 12. How do I report a problem or provide feedback?

Use the application's feedback or support feature, if available, or contact the appropriate support personnel for assistance.

## Privacy and Security

### 13. Is my information secure?

MyChild Helpline implements security measures to protect user information and limit access to authorized personnel only.

### 14. Who can access my account information?

Only authorized personnel and system administrators with appropriate permissions can access information necessary for system operations and support services.

### 15. How can I keep my account secure?

Use a strong password, avoid sharing your login credentials, and sign out of your account when using a shared device.
