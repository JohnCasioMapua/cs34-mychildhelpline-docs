# Set up and edit User Profile

## Overview

Your profile stores your name, age, gender, and location. The app uses this to show you the closest helpline numbers and child services available. 

## Setting Up Your Profile

1. Open the **MyChild Helpline** app.
2. Select **Get Started** on the welcome screen.
3. Enter your name in the **Name** field.
4. Select your age from the dropdown menu.
5. Select your gender from the options provided.
6. Select your country or region from the **Location** list.
7. Select **Save Profile**.

The app saves your profile and takes you to the home screen.

## Choosing an Avatar

![Avatar](../assets/avatar-profile.gif)

After saving your profile, the app prompts you to choose an avatar. Avatars appear on your profile and throughout the app.

1. Scroll through the available avatars on the **Choose Your Avatar** screen.
2. Select an avatar to preview it.
3. Select **Confirm** to save your choice.

You can change your avatar at any time from **Settings > Profile**.

## Updating Your Profile

1. Select **Settings** from the home screen.
2. Select **Profile**.
3. Select the field you want to update and enter the new value.
4. Select **Save Changes**.
