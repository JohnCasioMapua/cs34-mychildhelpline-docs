# Play Games in Arcade

## Overview

The **Arcade** is a collection of games in the app. It gives you a fun way to take a break. You can play alone against the app or challenge a friend on the same device. New games are added to the Arcade over time.

![Chill Spot](../assets/chill%20spot%20gif.gif)

## Opening the Arcade

1. Open the **MyChild Helpline** app.
2. Select **Arcade** on the home screen.

The Arcade screen opens and displays all available games.

## Starting a Game

1. Select a game from the **Arcade** screen.
2. Choose your game mode on the next screen:
   - Select **Play vs. App** to play against the app.
   - Select **Play vs. Friend** to play against another person on the same device.
3. Select **Start**.

The game begins immediately.

### Play vs. App mode

You play against the app. The app takes its turn after yours. 

### Play vs. Friend mode

You and a friend can take turns on the same device. The screen tells you whose turn it is.

![Arcade](../assets/arcade-full.gif)

## Exiting a Game

To exit your current game and return to the Arcade:

1. Select the **arrow** at the top left of the game screen.
