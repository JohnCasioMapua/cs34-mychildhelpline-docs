# Access MyChild Online Services

## Overview

The **MyChild Online Services** feature provides access to additional resources and support services, including workshops, informational articles, counseling-related activities, and other educational content available through the **MyChild Helpline** platform.

## Before You Begin

Make sure that:

* The **MyChild Helpline** app is installed on your device.
* Your device has an active internet connection.
* You have successfully opened the application.

## Opening MyChild Online Services

1. Open the **MyChild Helpline** app.
2. Select **MyChild Online Services** from the main menu.

The app displays the available online services and resources.

## Browsing Available Services

Review the list of available resources, which may include:

* Workshops
* Informational articles
* Counseling-related activities
* Other educational content

Select a service to view more information.

## Accessing a Service

1. Select the service or resource you want to view.
2. Read the article, review the workshop details, or participate in the available activity.

The selected content opens and becomes available for viewing.

## Returning to the Services Menu

Use the available navigation controls to return to the **MyChild Online Services** page and browse additional resources.

## Troubleshooting

| **Issue**                                    | **Recommended Solution**                                            |
| -------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Services do not load                         | Verify that your device is connected to the internet and try again. |
| Unable to access **MyChild Online Services** | Restart the application and reopen the feature.                     |
| Content does not display correctly           | Refresh the page or reopen the selected service.                    |
| Application becomes unresponsive             | Close and relaunch the application.                                 |

## Additional Notes

* Available services may vary depending on your location and supported country.
* New workshops, articles, and activities may be added periodically.
* An internet connection is required to access online resources and services.
