# Explore Resources

## Overview

The *Resources* section provides access to organizations and services that offer support in areas such as health, child protection, education, social welfare, libraries, and police services.

## Opening Resources

1. Open the *MyChild Helpline* app.
2. Select *Information Kiosk* from the bottom navigation bar.
3. Select *Resources*.

The app displays the available resource categories.

## Browsing Resource Categories

The *Resources* section includes the following categories:

* Health
* Child Protection
* Police & Related Services
* Library
* Social Welfare
* Education

Select a category to view the available organizations and services.

## Viewing Resource Information

Each category displays a list of related organizations.

Depending on the organization, you may see one or more of the following options:

* *Call*
* *Location*
* *Website*

The available options may vary.

## Calling an Organization

1. Select *Call* for the organization you want to contact.

The app opens your device's phone application and dials the organization's contact number.

## Viewing an Organization's Location

1. Select *Location*.

The app opens your device's map application and displays the organization's location.

## Visiting an Organization's Website

1. Select *Website*.

The app opens the organization's official website in your device's web browser.

## Returning to the Previous Screen

To return to the previous page:

1. Select the *Back* (<) icon in the upper-left corner of the screen.

The app returns you to the previous screen.
