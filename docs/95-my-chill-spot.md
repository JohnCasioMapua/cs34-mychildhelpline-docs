# Use My Chill Spot

## Overview

The **My Chill Spot** feature provides guided relaxation activities to help you feel calm, manage your emotions, and reduce stress. You can choose from simple exercises designed to promote relaxation and focus.

![My Chill Spot](../assets/my-chill-spot.gif)

## Opening My Chill Spot

1. Open the **MyChild Helpline** app.
2. Select **My Space** from the bottom navigation bar.
3. Select **My Chill Spot**.

**Helping Hand** welcomes you and introduces the available relaxation activities.

## Choosing a Relaxation Activity

The **My Chill Spot** screen offers the following activities:

* **Breath**
* **Counting from 10**

Select an activity to begin.

## Using the Breath Activity

1. Select **Breath**.

A guided breathing video plays on the screen. Follow the exercise at your own pace.

To watch the activity again:

1. Select **Replay** after the video finishes.

You can replay the exercise as many times as needed.

## Using the Counting from 10 Activity

1. Select **Counting from 10**.

A guided video plays, leading you through counting from 1 to 10 to help you relax.

To watch the activity again:

1. Select **Replay** after the video finishes.

You can replay the exercise whenever you want.

## Returning to the Previous Screen

To return to the previous page:

1. Select the **Back** (`<`) icon in the upper-left corner of the screen.

The app returns you to the previous screen.
