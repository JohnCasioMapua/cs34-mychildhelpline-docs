## Overview

The **Resources** section gives you direct access to UNICEF and ChildLine online materials without leaving the app. An active internet connection is required to load resources.

## Browsing Resources

1. Open the **MyChild Helpline** app.
2. Select **Resources** on the home screen.
3. Select a topic from the list.
4. Select a resource link to open it in the app's built-in browser.

## Changing the Language

1. Select the **language icon** at the top right of the **Resources** screen.
2. Select your preferred language.

Resources are available in English, Spanish, French, and Dutch.

> **Note:** If a resource is not available in your selected language, the app displays it in English.
