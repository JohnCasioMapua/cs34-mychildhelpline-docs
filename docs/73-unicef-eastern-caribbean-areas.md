# Exploring UNICEF Eastern Caribbean Area

## Overview

The UNICEF Eastern Caribbean Area page provides access to UNICEF news, programmes, stories, research, and donation opportunities. You can browse articles, learn about UNICEF initiatives, and support their work through the integrated website.

## Viewing the UNICEF Eastern Caribbean Area

1. Open the MyChild Helpline app.
2. Select **Information Kiosk** from the home screen.
3. Select **UNICEF Eastern Caribbean Area**.
4. The UNICEF Eastern Caribbean Area page opens and displays featured content, including news, programmes, and stories.

## Reading the Latest News

1. Scroll to the **Latest News** section.
2. Select a news article.
3. The full article opens and can be read by scrolling through the page.
4. Select the **Share** button at the bottom-right corner of the screen to share the article.
5. Choose your preferred sharing platform, such as **WhatsApp**, **LinkedIn**, **X**, or **Facebook**.

**Note:** Selecting the back arrow returns you to the MyChild Helpline home screen.

## Exploring UNICEF Programmes

1. Scroll to the **Our Programmes** section.
2. Select one of the available programmes, such as:
   - Child Participation
   - Transforming Education
   - Keeping Children Safe
   - Supporting Children in Need
   - Tackling Climate Change
   - Our Response in Emergencies
3. The selected programme page opens with detailed information about the initiative.
4. Use the **Jump to** links, when available, to navigate directly to sections such as **Challenge**, **Solution**, or **Resources**.
5. Scroll through the page to read the programme information.
6. When available, select downloadable overview documents to learn more about the programme's key priorities, challenges, resources, and regional initiatives.

**Note:** Selecting the back arrow returns you to the MyChild Helpline home screen.

## Reading UNICEF Stories

1. Scroll to the **Stories** section.
2. Select **Read the story** on the article you want to view.
3. Read the story by scrolling through the page.
4. Select the **Share** button to share the story using the available platforms.

## Searching for Information

1. Select the **Search** icon.
2. Enter a keyword or phrase into the search field.
3. Complete the required security verification, if prompted.
4. View the search results related to your query.

**Note:** Search results may take additional time to load depending on your internet connection and the verification process.

## Accessing the Navigation Menu

1. Select the **Menu** icon.
2. Browse the available navigation options, including:
   - What We Do
   - Research and Reports
   - Stories
   - Take Action
   - Explore UNICEF
   - Press Centre
   - Visit UNICEF Global
3. Select the desired menu item to open that section.

## Making a Donation

1. Select **Donate** or **Support UNICEF**.
2. Choose either **Monthly** or **One-Time** donation.
3. Select one of the suggested donation amounts or enter a custom amount.
4. Complete the required information, including:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Contact Number
   - Date of Birth
   - Address
5. Optionally select the checkbox to donate on behalf of your family.
6. Select **Continue** to proceed with the donation process.

**Note:** If you select a one-time donation, the website may encourage you to switch to a monthly donation. You may continue with your original one-time donation if you prefer.

## Sharing UNICEF Content

1. Select the **Share** button available on supported pages.
2. Choose one of the available sharing options:
   - WhatsApp
   - LinkedIn
   - X
   - Facebook
3. The selected application opens and allows you to share the current page or article.
