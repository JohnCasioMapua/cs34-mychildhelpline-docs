# Explore UNICEF Eastern Caribbean Area

## Overview

The **UNICEF Eastern Caribbean Area** section provides access to UNICEF news, programmes, stories, research, and donation opportunities. You can browse articles, learn about UNICEF initiatives, and support their work through the integrated website.

## Opening UNICEF Eastern Caribbean Area

1. Open the **MyChild Helpline** app.
2. Select **Information Kiosk** on the home screen.
3. Select **UNICEF Eastern Caribbean Area**.

The **UNICEF Eastern Caribbean Area** page opens and displays featured content, including news, programmes, and stories.

## Reading the Latest News

1. Scroll to the **Latest News** section.
2. Select a news article.

The full article opens and can be read by scrolling through the page.

To share the article:

1. Select the **Share** button in the lower-right corner of the screen.
2. Choose your preferred sharing platform, such as **WhatsApp**, **LinkedIn**, **X**, or **Facebook**.

> **Note:** Select the **Back** arrow to return to the previous page.

## Exploring UNICEF Programmes

1. Scroll to the **Our Programmes** section.
2. Select a programme to learn more about it.

The selected programme page opens with detailed information about the initiative.

You can:

* Use the **Jump To** links, when available, to navigate directly to sections such as **Challenge**, **Solution**, or **Resources**.
* Scroll through the page to read programme information.
* Open available overview documents to learn more about key priorities, challenges, and regional initiatives.

> **Note:** Select the **Back** arrow to return to the previous page.

## Reading UNICEF Stories

1. Scroll to the **Stories** section.
2. Select **Read the Story** on the article you want to view.

The story opens and can be read by scrolling through the page.

To share the story:

1. Select the **Share** button.
2. Choose one of the available sharing options.

## Searching for Information

1. Select the **Search** icon.
2. Enter a keyword or phrase into the search field.
3. Complete the required security verification, if prompted.

The page displays results related to your search query.

> **Note:** Search results may take additional time to load depending on your internet connection and the verification process.

## Using the Navigation Menu

1. Select the **Menu** icon.
2. Browse the available navigation options.
3. Select a menu item to open that section.

Available options may include:

* What We Do
* Research and Reports
* Stories
* Take Action
* Explore UNICEF
* Press Centre
* Visit UNICEF Global

## Making a Donation

1. Select **Donate** or **Support UNICEF**.
2. Choose **Monthly** or **One-Time** donation.
3. Select a suggested donation amount or enter a custom amount.
4. Complete the required information.
5. Select **Continue** to proceed with the donation process.

> **Note:** If you choose a one-time donation, the website may recommend switching to a monthly donation. You may continue with your original one-time donation if you prefer.

## Sharing UNICEF Content

1. Select the **Share** button on a supported page or article.
2. Choose your preferred sharing platform.

The selected application opens and allows you to share the current page or article.
