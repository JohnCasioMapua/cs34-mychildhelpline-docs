# MyChild HelpLine — My Diary User Guide

The **My Diary** feature is a private, secure digital space inside the MyChild HelpLine application. It is designed to give you a safe place to express your thoughts, process feelings, and keep a personal log that belongs to you alone. 

---

## Privacy and Safety First
Before you write your first entry, there are a few important things you should know about your privacy:

* **Your Words are Private:** Your diary entries are stored directly on your own mobile device. They are encrypted, meaning no one else can read them.
* **UNICEF Cannot Read Your Diary:** Staff, counselors, and system administrators cannot see what you write. 
* **Zero Personal Information:** The app does not link your diary entries to your real name, email address, or phone number.

---

## Setting Up Your Private PIN
To keep your diary safe from anyone who might pick up your phone, you must set up a private Personal Identification Number (PIN).

1. Open the **MyChild HelpLine** app and tap on the **My Diary** icon.
2. The app will prompt you to **Create a 4-Digit PIN**.
3. Type in a 4-digit number that is easy for you to remember, but hard for others to guess.
4. Re-enter the same 4-digit number to confirm it.
5. Choose a **Security Recovery Question** from the dropdown menu and type your secret answer. This helps you get back into your diary if you ever forget your PIN.

> ⚠️ **Important Security Note:** Never share your PIN with anyone. If you forget your PIN and your recovery answer, your past entries cannot be recovered by administrators, as the data is locked tightly for your safety.

---

## Creating and Managing Entries

Writing in your diary is simple and can be done at any time, even without an internet connection.

### How to Write a New Entry
1. Open the diary module and enter your **4-Digit PIN**.
2. Tap the **+ (Plus)** button at the bottom of the screen to open a fresh page.
3. Tap the title bar to give your entry a name, or simply start typing your thoughts in the main text area.
4. *Optional:* Tap the mood icon at the top of the entry to link your writing to how you are feeling that day.
5. Tap the **Save (Checkmark)** icon in the top-right corner to lock your entry.

### How to View, Edit, or Delete an Entry
* **To Read or Edit:** Tap on any past entry from your diary timeline list. Make your changes and tap **Save** when you are finished.
* **To Delete:** Open the entry you wish to remove, tap the **Trash Can** icon, and confirm by tapping **Delete**. Once an entry is deleted, it is permanently gone and cannot be restored.

---

## Quick Tips for Using Your Diary
* **Log Your Moods:** Try linking your diary entries to the app's Mood Tracker. It can help you notice patterns in how you feel over time.
* **Use It to Prepare for Chatting:** If you are planning to use the Live Chat feature to talk to a counselor, writing your thoughts down in the diary first can help you organize what you want to say.
