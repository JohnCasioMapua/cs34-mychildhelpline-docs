# Launch the MyChild Helpline app

## Overview

After installing the **MyChild Helpline** app, open it on your device to register a new account or sign in to an existing one.

## Launching the App

1. Locate the **MyChild Helpline** app on your device's home screen or app drawer.
2. Select the **MyChild Helpline** app icon to open it.
3. Wait for the app to finish loading.
4. Complete the account registration process or sign in to your existing account.

The home screen appears after you successfully register or sign in, allowing you to access the app's features.
