# Request a counseling session

## Overview

The **Counseling** feature connects you with a licensed counselor through an online appointment. If you need professional help, request a session.

![Request for Counselling](../assets/request-for-counselling.gif)

## Submitting a Session Request

1. Open the **MyChild Helpline** app.
2. Select **Counseling** on the home screen.
3. Select **Request a Session**.
4. Choose your preferred date from the calendar that appears.
5. Select an available time slot.
6. Enter your name in the **Full Name** field.
7. Enter a brief description of your concern in the **Reason for Session** field.
8. Select **Submit Request**.

A confirmation message appears on screen after you submit. The helpline team also sends a confirmation to your registered contact information.

## Joining Your Scheduled Session

1. Open the **MyChild Helpline** app at your scheduled time.
2. Select **My Sessions** on the home screen.
3. Select your upcoming session from the list.
4. Select **Join Session**.

The app connects you to your counselor. Allow the app to access your microphone when prompted.

> **Note:** Join the session at least two minutes before your scheduled time. The app may close the session slot if you do not connect within five minutes of the start time.

## Rescheduling or Canceling a Session

1. Select **My Sessions** on the home screen.
2. Select the session you want to change.
3. Select **Reschedule** or **Cancel Session**.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the change.

Cancel at least 24 hours before your session whenever possible. This allows another child to use the slot.
