# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### 1. What is psychosocial support?

Psychosocial support is the combination of psychological and social support that promotes the mental and emotional well-being of children and families.

### 2. What services does MyChild Helpline provide?

The app connects users to child-protection support services, including direct access to national toll-free hotlines, developmentally appropriate resources, and interactive activities.

### 3. Who can use MyChild Helpline?

The app provides remote mental health and psychosocial support and is accessible to children and caregivers.

### 4. Where is MyChild Helpline available?

In its first phase of implementation, the MyChild Helpline app is available in Antigua and Barbuda, Barbados, Grenada, and St. Vincent and the Grenadines.

### 5. Is my information kept confidential?

Yes. All information shared through the app is held in the strictest confidence and is only shared with appropriate personnel and agencies when absolutely necessary.

### 6. Is tele-counseling available on MyChild Helpline?

Yes. Tele-counseling services are available through the app at no charge.
